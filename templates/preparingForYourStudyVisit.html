location of your MRI scan...

parking...

Boston Children’s Hospital Waltham has two parking options available to patients and families:

Boston Children’s Hospital Waltham Parking Garage
This covered garage is located at the hospital entrance.
There is no charge to park in the garage.

Boston Children’s Hospital Waltham Parking Lot
This parking lot is located further down Hope Avenue, behind the hospital parking garage.
Parking in this outdoor lot is free of charge.

dress requirements...

In order to go inside the MRI room, you and your child must be metal-free.

On the day you are scheduled for your MRI appointment, we ask that you dress your child in clothing that does not contain any metal (such as snaps, zippers, belt or shoe buckles, hair clips, jewelry or earrings). We will check to make sure that you and your child are metal-free with a metal detector.   If you plan to stay inside the room with your child, we ask that you bring or wear metal-free clothing.

People with metal inside their bodies and pregnant women are also not allowed inside the MRI room. This includes:

  1. dental braces
  2. permanent dental retainers
  3. cochlear implant
  4. aneurysm clip
  5. metal fragments in the eye
  6. shrapnel
  7. neurostimulator
  8. cardiac pacemaker
  9. glasses with metal frames*
  10. body piercings
  11. some intrauterine devices - check with us!

*If you bring your prescription, we can provide you with a temporary pair of glasses that are MRI-safe.
